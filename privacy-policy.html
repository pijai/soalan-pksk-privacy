1. Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app collects minimal information necessary to provide the best learning experience:

  • App Usage Data: Progress tracking, question attempts, correct/incorrect answers, time spent on each subject
  • Device Information: Operating system version, device model for compatibility purposes
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or social media information
  • Financial information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Personalized Learning: Track progress, recommend topics, show performance analytics
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Technical Support: Debug issues and provide customer support
  • Research: Anonymous data for educational research and statistics

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We take data protection seriously. Here's our data sharing policy:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • Analytics Services: We may use anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, user data may be transferred

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • No Server Transmission: Personal data is not transmitted to external servers
  • App Security: Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored

5. Your Rights Under Malaysian Law

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal: Opt-out of data collection (may affect app functionality)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Information: Know how your data is used and shared

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

6. Children's Privacy

Soalan PKSK is designed for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians concerned about their child's use of this app should contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Progress Data: Retained until you delete the app or reset progress
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous data may be retained for statistical analysis
  • Error Reports: Kept for 30 days to resolve technical issues
  • User Request: Data deleted upon your request

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will:

  • Post the updated policy in the app
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users of significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@pijainett.com
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Policy Inquiry - Soalan PKSK"
  • Response Time: We will respond within 14 business days
